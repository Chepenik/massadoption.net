Boston's Freedom Festival

Join us in Boston for a Bitcoin-only festival celebrating the revolutionary power of Bitcoin. Enjoy delicious treats from food trucks that accept sats, such as Bon-mi and authentic Texas BBQ. Immerse yourself in informative talks and engaging discussions led by prominent Bitcoin speakers, while enjoying live music performances by local and international artists. Explore a wide variety of Bitcoin merchandise, from customized t-shirts to hardware wallets, and vintage clothing. Compete in 3 v 3 basketball competitions for the chance to earn some sats. Take in the beautiful New England summer as the backdrop to the festival, and seize the opportunity to explore the historic city of Boston, home to some of the most iconic landmarks of American history. The MassAdoption Freedom Festival is a chance to Connect with other Bitcoin enthusiasts, plebs, and hodlers alike, to share stories, ideas, and insights about the revolutionary power of Bitcoin while enjoying a memorable weekend filled with good times and great company.

The MassAdoption Festival in Boston offers a platform for Bitcoin enthusiasts to connect, network, and explore ways to promote mass adoption and sound money. The festival focuses on the importance of Bitcoin in solving global problems and encourages discussions on its impact. Attendees can enjoy live music, compete for sats, and celebrate the Bitcoin revolution, promoting a future where technology benefits everyone. The festival invites Bitcoin enthusiasts to make new connections, share ideas, and help drive the future of money towards greater freedom.
The event is taking place on June 24, 2023 at the Reggie Lewis Center in downtown Boston from 12PM - 6PM EST.
